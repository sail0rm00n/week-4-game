Crystal Collector


Rules


  1. You'll be given a random number at the start of the game, shown in "Number to Get".

  2. There are four Crystal Gems from Cartoon Network's Steven Universe below.

    • Garnet

    • Pearl

    • Amethyst

    • Lapis Lazuli

  3. By clicking on a Crystal Gem, you'll add a specific amount of points to your total score.

  4. Win the game by matching your total score to the random number!

  5. Lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

  6. The value of each Crystal Gem is hidden until you click on it.

  7. The game will change the values of each Crystal Gem every time you start the game. Good luck!
